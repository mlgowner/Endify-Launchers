About Us

Endify Launcher was born in 2023 when a group of passionate Minecraft enthusiasts decided to create a unique launcher combining the best features of VimeWorld and TLauncher. Our goal was to provide a seamless experience with a wide range of mods, servers, and modes, all wrapped in a sleek, user-friendly interface. Today, we continue to innovate and support the community!
